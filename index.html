Hi! I'm a post-bachelor student researcher at Los Alamos National Laboratory. I recently graduated as a mathematics student in the Plan II honors program at the University of Texas at Austin. Before that, I worked at ARL:UT, the Oden Institute, and the Library of Congress's John W. Kluge Center. My research interests include mathematical theory in machine learning, ML for scientific computing, and AI for cultural institutions.
